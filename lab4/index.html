Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem Solving

Challenge

The challenge of this lab is to familiarize ourselves with pseudocoding tasks in order to help us organize our code.

Problems

While my partner and I did not experience any major problems for this lab, we found it to be a bit challenging to think of high-level tasks when pseudocoding for the computer-game we chose (connect four).

Reflection

This lab was quite effective at teaching us the importance of creating an outline for our work before diving head-first into an assignment. By practicing pseudocoding, we learned how to break down complex problems into more managable tasks that are easier to navigate through. Doing this improves the clarity of our approach to problems.

Results

We successfully provided a pseudocode for an everyday task, like making spaghetti, as well as a possible pseudocode for a computer game, connect-four.
